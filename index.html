Welcome to Tutor.me

Tutor.me is an online social network platform that allows
users to connect with an online tutoring community to find
other community members willing to tutor or help with their
classes. Tutor.me is geared towards bring people together with
the goal to help tutor those who are struggling with theit classes.
With Tutor.me users are able to find others who either have the knowledge
they need to pass their classes or find others in the community
who also are taking the same classes.
